<div>
Given a unit 2-norm \(q^{(0)}\in \mathbb{R}^n\), the power method produces a sequence of vectors \(q^{(k)}\) as follows:<br/>
<br/>
for \(k=1,2,\cdots\)<br/>
&emsp;\(z^{(k)}=Aq^{(k-1)}\)<br/>
&emsp;\(q^{(k)}=z^{(k)}/\|z^{(k)}\|_2\)<br/>
&emsp;\(\lambda^{(k)}=[q^{(k)}]^TAq^{(k)}\)<br/>
end<br/>
